# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

- Always be polite and professional in all communications.
- Flag any payment over $500 for human approval.
- Prioritize urgent messages from clients.
- Maintain confidentiality of all sensitive information.

## Communication Guidelines

- **Email:** Respond to client emails within 24 hours.
- **WhatsApp:** Check for urgent messages every 30 minutes.
- **LinkedIn:** Post business updates twice a week.

## Approval Workflow

- All outgoing payments require human approval by moving the corresponding file from `/Pending_Approval` to `/Approved`.
- Sensitive social media posts (e.g., replies, direct messages) require human approval.

## System Maintenance

- Regularly review logs in `/Vault/Logs` for errors.
- Conduct a weekly business and accounting audit.
